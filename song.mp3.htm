YouTube to MP3 Converter

With our YouTube to MP3 Converter (shortly YTMP3), you are able to convert your favorite YouTube videos to MP3 (audio) or MP4 (video) files for free. YTMP3 is designed to work on both mobile and desktop devices without the installation of any app.

How to use our YouTube to MP3 Converter:

1. Open YouTube.com and search for a video you would like to download.

2. Once you have found a video, you need to copy the video URL. The simplest way is to copy the video URL directly from your browser's address bar. Please note: The video must be playing to obtain the correct URL.

3. Paste the video URL into our YouTube converter and choose your download format. By default, the video will be converted to an MP3 (audio) file.

4. Click the „Convert” button to start the conversion of the YouTube video. The conversion may take a few moments. Please note: Only videos with a maximum length of 90 minutes can be converted.

5. Once the conversion is complete, you can download the converted file by clicking the „Download” button.
